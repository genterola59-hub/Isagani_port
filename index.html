About Me

Passionate developer with expertise in both front-end and back-end technologies

Hello! I'm Isagani Genterola

I'm a passionate Web Developer with strong experience in both front-end and back-end development. I specialize in creating functional, user-friendly, and responsive web systems that solve real-world problems.

I have solid skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, with experience building complete systems from the database to the user interface. On the front end, I focus on delivering clean, professional, and responsive designs. On the back end, I ensure smooth data flow and secure functionality.

Beyond coding, I have a background in technical support, networking (NC II certified), and hardware troubleshooting, which gives me a unique perspective in developing reliable and efficient systems. I also have knowledge in graphic design and layout creation using Adobe Illustrator, Canva, and Photoshop, allowing me to blend functionality with creative visuals.

I'm always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenges that push me to grow as a developer.
